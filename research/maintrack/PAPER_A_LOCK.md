# Paper A Lock

Paper A is the VERA method paper. Science-fair framing is out of scope.

## Locked Thesis

VERA converts representation erasure from an IID edit-selection problem into a
shift-scoped certification problem. For every registered edit, it reports a
simultaneous lower confidence bound on the largest bounded density-ratio shift
under which paired target harm and every registered post-edit leakage contract
remain valid. It returns `ABSTAIN` when the IID contract cannot be established
or when deployment includes support absent from certification.

## Contribution Sentence

VERA is a shift-aware decision layer over representation interventions. INLP,
R-LACE, LEACE, TaCo, and MANCE++ construct candidate edits; VERA lower-certifies
their groupwise paired-harm and heterogeneous-attacker **representation-edit shift
envelope**, with the common shift radius as its minimum coordinate.
The finite-family testing mechanism is attributed to Learn Then Test and Pareto
Testing, while the proposed contribution is the edit-specific robust object,
its support boundary, its prospective minimax additive evidence allocation,
and the evidence that the distinction changes deployment decisions. The locked
primary study uses the earlier square-score allocation; a pre-outcome extension
tests the full additive allocator without replacing any primary gate.

## Evidence Lock

The evidence package consists of exact small-case enumeration, a 54-cell exact
balanced study, a 216-cell candidate/group-count extension, a 200-run
confirmation, an 800-run disjoint IID replication, and a prospective 1,280-run
controlled supported-shift matrix over seeds 45--108. Every real run has a
compact receipt tied to a committed preregistration, pinned upstream code, split
hashes, and per-example audit arrays. In the controlled study, a separate
design fold may choose the supported shift cell and evidence allocation, but
certification and final shifted-law streams remain untouched. Aggregate
confirmatory analysis follows the locked seed-cluster endpoints only after the
full receipt audit.
