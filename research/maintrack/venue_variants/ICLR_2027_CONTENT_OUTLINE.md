# ICLR 2027 Scientific Content Outline

Status: outcome-blind outline; official 2027 format pending

Working title: **VERA: Evidence-Efficient Certification of Representation Edits
Under Deployment Shift**

The title changes to **Support-Aware Certification** if the frozen
evidence-efficient title-retention rule fails.

## Abstract

Lead with representation editing as an intervention whose downstream target
effect and residual recoverability can change under reweighting. Define VERA's
vector shift envelope, common-radius summary, and abstention boundary. Attribute
finite-family testing to Learn Then Test. Report the IID uncertainty result and
controlled Gamma-greater-than-one result separately, then state the
Camelyon17 support boundary. Use only shared audited result macros.

## 1. Representation Edits Need Deployment Decisions

Frame INLP, R-LACE, LEACE, TaCo, and MANCE++ as candidate constructors. Motivate
paired intervention harm, heterogeneous retrained attackers, and whole-edit
abstention. State exactly four shared contributions and make clear that VERA is
not a new eraser or universal information-removal guarantee.

## 2. Closest Work

Cover concept erasure, causal/amnesic probing, fairness certificates, Learn Then
Test, Pareto Testing, Conformal Risk Control, Prompt Risk Control, robust
validation, and selective prediction. Use the 16-capability matrix in the
supplement and position the additive allocator against the final primary-source
allocation search. Include the three short novelty examples in prose.

## 3. Representation-Intervention Contract

Define frozen representation, identity intervention, edited representation,
retrained target learner, paired target harm, source-balanced attacker recall,
environment and source conditionals, registered attacker portfolio, support,
and declared density-ratio profiles. Explain why one native probe, ordinary
accuracy, or a scalar average does not express the required contract.

## 4. VERA Envelope and Decision

Present the fixed-profile IUT, simultaneous component curves, anisotropic
envelope, common radius, limiting contracts, and unsupported coordinate rule.
Show Figure 1 early. Explain that utility selection may occur among candidates
already covered by the same simultaneous event.

## 5. Guarantees and Evidence Design

State the fixed-profile, vector-envelope, continuum, mixture, source-invariance,
sample-size, additive allocation, lower-bound, and unsupported-cell results.
Keep full proofs in the supplement but include assumptions and proof ideas. Make
the separate target/source stream architecture and the lack of globally optimal
joint stratified collection explicit.

## 6. Controlled Cross-Modal Study

Describe the four supported datasets, separate Camelyon17 boundary, five pinned
upstream entry points, 12-candidate frontier, four registered attackers,
held-out boosted tree, 64 seeds, construction/design/certification/evaluation
separation, exact finite-reference reweighting, known ambiguity membership, and
nine matched deployment rules. State all thresholds, Gamma values, budgets,
allocations, tests, and failure rules.

## 7. Deployment Safety and Useful Abstention

Lead with the primary controlled result whether favorable or unfavorable. Show
the one shared nine-rule table and three-panel result figure. Report exact
violation effects, safety scope, safe retention, vector/common advantage,
deployment rate, common radii, and limiting coordinates. Follow with the prior
IID result and its failed auxiliary Gait severity gate.

## 8. What Parts of VERA Matter?

Give ICLR more space than AAAI for the 16 registered ablations: paired versus
unpaired target error, balanced versus ordinary leakage, native versus retrained
attackers, portfolio composition, IID versus shifted certification, scalar
versus vector, common versus anisotropic, support check, multiplicity, evidence
allocation, candidate screening/family size, environment count, evidence size,
exact versus generic bounds, and targeted collection. Keep all negative
ablations.

## 9. Evidence Growth and Failure Diagnosis

Report uniform, square-score, and additive allocations on disjoint streams,
plus cell-level planning estimates and oracle-only benchmark explanations. Give
the full GaitPDB limiting-contract diagnosis and held-out-attacker challenge.
Remove `Evidence-Efficient` from the title if the frozen rule requires it.

## 10. Limitations, Responsible Use, and Conclusion

State finite-reference, support, portfolio, target-learner, threshold-design,
algorithmic-seed, separate-stream, and nonclinical limits. Explain that a
certificate is a conditional deployment statement, not privacy or fairness.
Include the concise full-scope AI-assistance disclosure and human-responsibility
statement required by the final official policy.

## ICLR Supplement

Include authoritative proofs, exact enumeration, theorem-to-code mapping,
official adapter boundaries, all controlled tables, all 16 ablations, allocation
solver checks, evidence top-ups, Gait diagnosis, held-out attacker results,
negative results, licenses, canonical receipts, and reproduction instructions.
