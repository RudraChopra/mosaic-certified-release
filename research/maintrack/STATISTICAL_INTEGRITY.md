# VERA Statistical Integrity

## Receipt And Inferential Units

The receipt unit is one preregistered dataset, official eraser, and untouched
algorithmic seed. The balanced confirmatory matrix contains five
datasets, five pinned official erasers, and seeds 5 through 12, for 200 JSON
receipts. The independent stress replication repeats the official-code matrix
on disjoint seeds 13 through 44, for 800 additional JSON receipts. Every receipt
must match the locked preregistration hash, runner commit, upstream commit,
split hashes, and per-example audit-array hashes. A missing, proxy, dirty, or
checksum-mismatched cell fails closed.

For the prospective controlled-shift study, the primary independent unit is the
whole seed cluster across four supported datasets, not a receipt, candidate,
attacker, profile, or dataset decision. Seeds 45 through 108 produce 1,280
official-method receipts but only 64 independent primary clusters.

Seeds 0 through 4 informed estimand repair, threshold-family design, and power
diagnostics. They are exploratory and contribute to no confirmatory estimate,
interval, hypothesis test, or abstract number.

## Selection And Outcomes

In the prior IID studies, edits, target probes, leakage attackers,
certification bounds, and deployment choices use no external labels. External
arrays are generated only after each candidate is fixed and are used for locked
evaluation. Balanced-confirmatory aggregate analysis begins only after all 200
runs finish and the receipt audit passes. Independent-stress aggregate analysis
begins only after all 800 replication runs finish and the independent receipt
audit passes.

In the controlled study, a disjoint design fold may use metadata to choose the
supported focus cell and outcomes to compute the prospective evidence
allocation. It may not fit edits, target probes, or attackers; read
certification-stream or final-stream outcomes; change candidates, contracts, or
rules; or determine a primary violation label. Certification samples and the
50,000-draw final stream are independently generated after the design is fixed.
Primary contract status uses the exact shifted finite-law expectation.
Aggregate outcome access begins only after all 1,280 receipts pass audit and
the outcome-blind interpretation and analysis specifications are committed.

The balanced confirmatory analysis uses the full certification fold, all nine
registered target-harm/leakage threshold pairs, and Gamma 1.0. Gamma 1.01 is a
fixed shift sensitivity. The independent stress replication uses one locked
contract per supported dataset, selected only from design seeds 5 through 12 and
frozen before seeds 13 through 44. No alternate seed, threshold, validation
fraction, attacker portfolio, or shift budget may replace an unfavorable
primary result.

## Dependence And Inference

Threshold pairs and nested validation fractions reuse the same examples and
seed-level fits. Configuration-level Clopper-Pearson and McNemar calculations
are therefore dependent-cell diagnostics. They are not treated as independent
replications.

The balanced confirmatory point-selection comparison averages all nine threshold
outcomes within each seed and uses a one-sided exact sign-flip test over the
eight seed blocks, with Holm correction over the four externally estimable
datasets. The independent stress replication uses exact one-sided paired
McNemar tests over 32 disjoint seed pairs per supported dataset, with Holm
correction over the same four externally estimable datasets. These tests rely on
paired sign symmetry. A sign-only test is reported as a lower-power sensitivity
without magnitude symmetry. Retention uncertainty uses whole-seed cluster
resampling; configuration-level binomial intervals are kept only as labeled
diagnostics.

The controlled primary paired comparison aggregates all four supported dataset
violations within each seed and applies a two-sided exact sign test to nonzero
seed differences. Safety uses one prespecified rotating dataset sentinel per
seed. Although sentinel probabilities may differ, the registered safety gate
can pass only at zero events; AM--GM makes that zero-event upper limit
conservative for average sentinel risk. Usefulness and deployment uncertainty
resample whole seed clusters. Per-dataset tests are secondary and Holm adjusted.
Candidates, attackers, evidence budgets, and shift profiles are correlated
analyses, not extra replicates.

## Claim Boundaries

Measured external violations are benchmark outcomes, not evidence that the
external benchmark belongs to the theorem's density-ratio class. Camelyon17
center 2 is outside certification support and therefore forces procedural
abstention. Its one-class external source slice makes balanced leakage
non-estimable and is never recoded as a measured violation.

The controlled central result has a different population: a finite empirical
reference law and exact supported reweightings whose density-ratio membership
is verified by construction. It is not population extrapolation to new people,
images, comments, recordings, hospitals, or domains. The independent
50,000-draw stream is a Monte Carlo replay and cannot replace or alter the exact
finite-law primary labels.

Across every study, all registered datasets and methods, every adjusted
p-value, and every failed pass condition remain in the released artifacts
regardless of outcome.
