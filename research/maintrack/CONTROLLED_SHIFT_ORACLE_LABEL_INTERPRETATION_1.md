# Controlled-Shift Oracle Label: Interpretation 1

This outcome-blind interpretation clarifies one presentation label without
changing a study object. The locked machine key `external_oracle` remains
unchanged for backward compatibility with the preregistration, analyzer, result
schema, and receipt fields.

For the seeds 45--108 controlled study, that key means the exact expectation
under the constructed finite shifted law `Q` over the registered reference
atoms. Submission-facing text and display items must therefore call it the
**exact shifted-law opportunity oracle** or, where space is tight, the
**finite-law opportunity oracle**. It must not be called external-population,
external-distribution, held-out-domain, or real-deployment validation.

The oracle defines exact contract-violation labels and the denominator of
oracle-safe opportunities only after the candidate family, shifted law,
contracts, and deployment rules are fixed. It is not a deployable rule, is not
used to fit an edit or certify a candidate, and carries no inference beyond the
finite registered law. The independent 50,000-draw stream remains a Monte
Carlo replay of that law and cannot alter an exact primary label.

Historical uses of external evaluation or an external oracle in the prior IID
studies keep their original meaning and labels. This interpretation changes no
candidate, draw, shift, threshold, rule, endpoint, numerator, denominator,
interval, gate, or title condition.
