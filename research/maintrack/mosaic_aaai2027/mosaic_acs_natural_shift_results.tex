\paragraph{Natural geographic transfer.}
The locked ACS study contains 60 jobs: three tasks, four target states, and
five seeds. At the primary $.40$ error contract, $K=4$ MOSAIC releases 15/60
jobs with 0/15 held-out population diagnostic violations. Releases cover all
four target states and two tasks. The direct target-table rule releases 47/60
with 0/47 held-out violations. At $K=8$, MOSAIC abstains on all 60 jobs while
the direct rule again releases 47/60. The larger token table therefore raises
the confidence cost enough to eliminate MOSAIC retention at the available
sample size.
