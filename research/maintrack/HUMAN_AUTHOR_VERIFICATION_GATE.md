# VERA Human-Author Verification Gate

This gate must be completed by every listed human author before submission.
Automation cannot mark any item complete. Passing software audits does not
establish authorship, understanding, proof correctness, or research integrity.

## Scientific Understanding

- Explain the difference between the LTT-style fixed-profile IUT and VERA's
  simultaneous support-aware shift envelope without reading the manuscript.
- Derive the bounded-density-ratio robust risk for Bernoulli leakage and paired
  harm on `{-1, 0, 1}`.
- Reproduce the fixed-profile false-acceptance proof and state why no
  multiplicity correction is used across components inside one candidate.
- Reproduce the unsupported-cell two-world proof and state exactly what it does
  and does not show about Camelyon17.
- Explain why balanced leakage is source-prior invariant but does not prove
  universal concept erasure or ordinary-accuracy control.

## Empirical Understanding

- Inspect the locked preregistrations and verify every hash sidecar.
- Trace one raw NPZ through certificate construction, candidate selection, and
  external evaluation by hand or with an independently written notebook.
- Explain the five deployment rules, nine threshold pairs, eight untouched
  seeds, fixed `Gamma=1.01` sensitivity, and abstention semantics.
- Verify the 200 official-code receipts and upstream repository pins.
- Recalculate the abstract's three numbers from the frozen rule rows.
- Explain why seed-blocked inference is primary and configuration-level
  McNemar/Clopper-Pearson calculations are only diagnostics.
- Inspect every final figure and table against its source artifact.

## Literature And Claims

- Read the cited Learn Then Test, Pareto Testing, Conformal Risk Control, robust
  validation, distributional fairness, and concept-erasure papers most central
  to the novelty boundary.
- Check every bibliography entry against the primary source.
- Confirm that the paper does not claim invention of finite-family acceptance,
  generic risk control, generic robust fairness auditing, or guaranteed
  external-distribution validity.
- Resolve all critical and major findings from four role-specific external cold
  reviewers and one fresh post-revision reviewer who publish in machine
  learning.

## Policy And Authorship

- Confirm that every listed author satisfies the target venue's authorship and
  accountability requirements.
- Confirm that the generative-AI disclosure is complete and accurate.
- Verify data licenses, ethical statements, anonymity, concurrent-submission
  rules, account requirements, and deadlines from official sources.
- Personally approve the final PDF, supplement, checklist, and anonymous ZIP by
  content hash.

Only after these steps are genuinely complete may the private submission
registry set `scientific_content_human_verified`,
`authorship_criteria_human_confirmed`, and
`ai_assistance_disclosure_human_confirmed` to `true`.
