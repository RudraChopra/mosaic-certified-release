# VERA Final Literature Refresh Specification

This search protocol is fixed before aggregate controlled-study outcome access.
It is not a preregistered empirical endpoint. Its purpose is to prevent a
favorable result from relaxing the prior-work standard or a weak result from
prompting selective novelty claims.

## Source Rule

Use primary papers and official proceedings, journal, OpenReview, arXiv, or
author-project pages. Search engines and bibliographic indexes may discover a
paper but do not support a manuscript claim by themselves. Record the final
search date, query, direct primary URL, title, authors, year, venue or status,
and the exact paper section supporting each coded capability.

## Certificate Queries

Run combinations of the following terms:

- `representation edit certification deployment shift`
- `concept erasure risk control distribution shift`
- `representation erasure abstention certificate`
- `paired intervention harm robust validation`
- `attacker portfolio certification representation`
- `vector robustness radius density ratio confidence`
- `support mismatch impossibility risk certification`
- `post selection ambiguity set confidence envelope`
- `conformal risk control group conditional shift`
- `distributional fairness certificate reweighting`

Search title, abstract, and full text where available. Follow backward and
forward citations from Learn Then Test, Pareto Testing, Conformal Risk Control,
Prompt Risk Control, Robust Validation, robust conformal inference,
distributional-fairness certification, FARE, MMD-B-Fair, and the five eraser
families used in the study.

## Evidence-Allocation Queries

Run combinations of:

- `active hypothesis testing multiple constraints sample allocation`
- `risk certification adaptive sample allocation`
- `conformal calibration active data collection`
- `optimal computing budget allocation feasibility testing`
- `Neyman allocation multiple endpoints confidence bounds`
- `best arm identification multiple constraints fixed budget`
- `stratified sampling worst normalized variance allocation`
- `active testing safety certification distribution shift`
- `experimental design ambiguity set radius certification`
- `sequential design intersection union testing`

Follow citation neighbors for optimal computing-budget allocation, Neyman and
stratified allocation, ranking-and-selection, fixed-budget best-arm
identification, thresholding bandits, active hypothesis testing, and sequential
multiple testing. Do not code generic inverse-variance or inverse-margin
allocation as absent merely because it uses different application language.

## Inclusion And Coding

Include every primary paper that plausibly supplies one of the 16 certificate
capabilities in `NOVELTY_MATRIX_2026-07-14.md`, the same prospective allocation
objective, a stronger adaptive version, or an equivalent support-boundary
result. For each paper record:

1. formal estimand and output;
2. whether candidates or constraints are finite or adaptive;
3. shift class and support assumptions;
4. simultaneous and post-selection validity;
5. target-deployment data requirement;
6. intervention pairing and attacker treatment;
7. vector, radius, abstention, and support outputs;
8. allocation objective, information sharing, and optimality statement;
9. empirical domain and matched baselines; and
10. exact overlap, difference, and manuscript correction required.

Use `Y`, `P`, and `--` conservatively. A capability that could be constructed by
combining two papers is not assigned to either paper, but the combination must
still be discussed as a novelty threat.

## Collision Rules

The current claim must be narrowed or reframed if a prior paper already reports
the full paired edit certificate or an equivalent object under another name.
The evidence-efficient title must be reconsidered if prior work supplies the
same certificate-specific allocation objective and guarantee, or if VERA's
allocation reduces to a standard cited result without a substantive new
interaction with shared target/attacker cells.

No internal search can close novelty. The final matrix, source-support ledger,
and paper wording must be reviewed by the four cold external roles. Record
uncertainty and unresolved collisions rather than converting absence from a
query into proof of novelty.
